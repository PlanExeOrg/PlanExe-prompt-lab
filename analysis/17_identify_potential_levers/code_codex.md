## Summary

The biggest code-level problems here are contract mismatches and harness drift, not chat-history assembly bugs. `identify_potential_levers.py` asks the model for some fields in one format and validates or consumes them in another, while `prompt_optimizer/runner.py` is less fault-tolerant than the real pipeline and does no upfront model validation. Those choices cleanly explain the three most actionable failures in the insight files: summary noncompliance, repeated gemini config blowups, and unrecovered EOF truncation. The best next fixes are to align the schema with the actual prompt contract, slim the per-call payload, validate models before the batch starts, and add consequence-field repair plus proper retry behavior in the optimizer path.
