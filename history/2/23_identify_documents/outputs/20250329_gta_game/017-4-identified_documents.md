### 2. Risk Management and Mitigation Framework

**ID:** 37fcdc42-e57e-4831-bac7-bdd8dad93e62

**Description:** A comprehensive framework document identifying all material risks (technical, financial, operational, regulatory, supply chain, security, and social) across the game development lifecycle. For each risk, it documents risk description, probability assessment, impact severity, mitigation strategies with specific SMART actions, responsible owners, and monitoring mechanisms. This framework operationalizes the high-level risks identified in the project plan with concrete, measurable mitigation activities and assigns accountability.

**Responsible Role Type:** Risk and Compliance Officer

**Primary Template:** ISO 31000 Risk Management Framework

**Secondary Template:** PMI Risk Management Plan Template

**Steps:**

- Conduct a comprehensive risk identification workshop with cross-functional team (technical leads, financial officer, operations, compliance)
- Categorize risks by domain: technical (procedural generation integration, performance optimization, AI bias), financial (funding partnership failures, cost overruns), operational (multi-location team coordination, personnel turnover), regulatory (content restrictions, GDPR/CCPA compliance), supply chain (GPU procurement, component availability), security (data breaches, IP theft), and social (content controversies, stakeholder conflicts)
- Assign probability and impact ratings to each identified risk using a standard matrix
- Develop specific, SMART mitigation strategies for high-priority risks with clear ownership and timelines
- Establish key risk indicators (KRIs) and monitoring mechanisms for ongoing risk tracking
- Define escalation procedures and contingency responses for each critical risk

**Approval Authorities:** Risk and Compliance Officer, Project Manager, Executive Sponsor
