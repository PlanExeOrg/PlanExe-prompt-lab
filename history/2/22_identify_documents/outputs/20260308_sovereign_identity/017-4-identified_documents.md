
## Documents to Create

### 1. Project Charter

**ID:** 117928ae-463a-4db2-9bd8-9079d5e99c07

**Description:** Formal document initiating the project, defining its objectives, scope, stakeholders, and high-level responsibilities. It serves as a reference point throughout the project lifecycle. Audience: All project stakeholders.

**Responsible Role Type:** Project Manager

**Primary Template:** PMI Project Charter Template

**Steps:**

- Define project goals and objectives based on the goal statement.
- Identify key stakeholders and their roles.
- Outline project scope, deliverables, and success criteria.
- Establish high-level budget and timeline.
- Obtain approval from Digitaliseringsstyrelsen (or relevant authority).

**Approval Authorities:** Digitaliseringsstyrelsen

### 2. Technical Feasibility Strategy Framework

**ID:** aa578419-f49d-4860-88b9-3599d27a3251

**Description:** A high-level framework outlining the approach to demonstrating the technical feasibility of platform-neutral and fallback-capable authentication. It defines the scope of the technical demonstrators, success metrics, and strategic choices. Audience: Technical team, Digitaliseringsstyrelsen.

**Responsible Role Type:** Technical Lead

**Steps:**

- Define the scope of the technical demonstrators (FIDO2, browser-based fallback).
- Establish success metrics for the demonstrators (completion, acceptance by reviewers).
- Outline strategic choices (single method vs. multiple methods).
- Identify potential risks and mitigation strategies.
- Incorporate findings from existing technical reports and standards.

**Approval Authorities:** Lead Researcher

### 7. Risk Register

**ID:** b0d57126-e1c7-403f-bc16-3c0df135270b

**Description:** A comprehensive register of all identified project risks, their likelihood, impact, and mitigation strategies. It is a living document that is updated throughout the project lifecycle. Audience: Project team, stakeholders.

**Responsible Role Type:** Project Manager

**Primary Template:** PMI Risk Register Template

**Steps:**

- Identify potential risks based on project plan, SWOT analysis, and expert input.
- Assess the likelihood and impact of each risk.
- Develop mitigation strategies for each risk.
- Assign responsibility for monitoring and managing each risk.
- Regularly review and update the risk register.

**Approval Authorities:** Lead Researcher
