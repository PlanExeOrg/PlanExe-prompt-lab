## Issue 1 - Lack of Detailed Financial Sustainability Plan
The plan lacks a concrete strategy for securing additional funding. Long-term viability depends on securing funds. Without a plan, the project risks being abandoned.

Recommendation: Develop a financial sustainability plan:

- Identify grant opportunities.
- Create a detailed budget.
- Establish metrics for fundraising.
- Assign fundraising responsibility.
- Create a sensitivity analysis of ROI based on funding.

Sensitivity: Failure to secure funding could reduce ROI by 20-30%. Securing 5 million DKK could extend the timeline by 12 months and increase ROI by 10-15%.

## Issue 2 - Insufficiently Defined Success Metrics and Milestones
The plan doesn't specify metrics, making it difficult to track progress. Without clear metrics, the project risks drifting off course.

Recommendation: Define SMART milestones for each phase:

- Phase 1: Complete regulatory due diligence by month 3.
- Phase 2: Develop a working FIDO2 demonstrator by month 12.
- Phase 3: Secure a formal response from Digitaliseringsstyrelsen by month 18.

Implement a project management framework. Conduct regular stakeholder reviews.

Sensitivity: A 3-month delay in Phase 2 could delay completion by 3-6 months and reduce ROI by 5-10%. Completing milestones ahead of schedule could increase ROI by 5-10%.

## Issue 3 - Unclear Stakeholder Engagement Strategy
The plan lacks a detailed strategy for engaging with stakeholders. Success depends on securing buy-in. Without a strategy, the project risks resistance.

Recommendation: Develop a stakeholder engagement strategy:

- Identify key stakeholders and their interests.
- Establish communication channels.
- Conduct regular meetings.
- Tailor messaging.
- Build relationships with decision-makers.

Sensitivity: Failure to secure buy-in from Digitaliseringsstyrelsen could reduce influence by 50-75%. Securing strong support could increase influence.

## Review conclusion
The plan lacks detail in financial sustainability, success metrics, and stakeholder engagement. Addressing these is crucial for long-term viability.
